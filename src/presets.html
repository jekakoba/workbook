QuestionsAND ANSWERS

What are Lightroom presets? 

Lightroom presets, also known as filters, are ready-made settings for colors and lighting. They quickly make your photos look more professional with just a single click. Without presets, editing photos in Lightroom can be time-consuming because of all the adjustments needed to achieve the perfect result.

Do I need to pay for Adobe Lightroom?

The mobile version is totally FREE to download. While there's a paid option for Lightroom mobile with extra features, you don't need it at all to use the presets.
